WORLD OF PURNEA

Purnia has an area of 3,202 square km.
It is a level, depressed tract of country, consisting for the most part of a rich, loamy soil of alluvial formation.
It is traversed by several rivers flowing from the Himalayas, which afford great advantages of irrigation and water-carriage.
